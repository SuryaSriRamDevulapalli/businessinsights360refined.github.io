This is Business Insights 360 Project

The goal of the project was to build an automated dashboard for the finance, sales, marketing, and supply chain teams at Atliq, to help them analyze the data effectively for better decision making.

Business Issue

It is a consumer goods electronics company having operations in various countries.Their business is growing rapidly and they still rely on excel files for data analytics. Excel files are hard to consume and not effective in generating insights. Also due to the lack of effective analytics the company faced a major loss in Latin America.Senior executives of this company have decided to invest in a data analytics project and have assigned a team for this work.

They want this team to show the insights on, Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain.
